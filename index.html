Kasey creates feminist art through an exploration of fluidity in gender. Her focus is on strong, fierce, unique women. Women in art are often depicted as soft, vulnerable, and nude; their gaze cast away from the viewer. The women Kasey creates captivate the viewer with their intense, unsmiling and unabashed stare.

Anime, body modification, and natural elements are key themes in her work. She utilizes blank or basic backgrounds to bring focus to her characters.
